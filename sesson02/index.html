Surely Non Alcoholic Sparkling Rosé

NON-ALCOHOLIC SPARKLING ROSE: This is not just sparkling grape juice or an imitation. Surely is made in partnership with the finest winemakers in Sonoma, California. We make a high quality wine the traditional way, then simply remove the alcohol. Wine and cocktail lovers, this is your new indulgence.

TASTING NOTES: This IWSC 2022 award-winning rose combines soft strawberry, pear, and tropical fruit flavors balanced with a light acid for a full finish. Pairs well with delicately smoked fish, decadent sushi, and a thick slice of cheesecake.

CLEAN LABEL: Made with natural ingredients, and wellness in mind, our non alcoholic sparkling wine checks off all the dietary boxes. Our rose is gluten free, vegan, 20 calories and 2g of sugar per serving.

MADE FOR HAPPIER HOURS: Whatever reason you have for choosing an alternative beverage option, our goal is to satisfy that choice. We want to give you the best of both worlds: great-tasting wines that don’t compromise your health and allow you to stay social.

WHY SURELY: For too long, opting out of alcohol has come with a social stigma. You shouldn’t feel weird or left out if you’re not drinking alcohol and you deserve to enjoy a highly crafted alternative option. We’re committed to offering a social drink that can sustain and improve your productivity and health goals, long term.
